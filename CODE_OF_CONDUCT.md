# Code of Conduct

We expect everyone in this project to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Make room for different backgrounds, experience levels, and opinions.
- Keep feedback specific and useful.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or threats
- insulting or demeaning language
- trolling, stalking, or doxxing
- repeated disruption of discussion or collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit content, reject contributions, or take other reasonable action when behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a problem, contact the project maintainers through the security or project contact channel listed in this repository.
